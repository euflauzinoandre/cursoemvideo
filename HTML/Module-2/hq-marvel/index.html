Peter Parker (voiced by Yuri Lowenthal) is a 23-year-old research assistant, who gains superhuman abilities after being bitten by a genetically modified spider. Assuming a secret identity as the superhero Spider-Man, Peter uses these abilities to protect the residents of New York City.
Born in the late 1800s, his early life involved a traumatic event where his mutant abilities manifested, leading him to adopt the name Logan and eventually join the Weapon X program. There, his skeleton was infused with adamantium, enhancing his abilities and solidifying his place as a key member of the X-Men as Wolverine.
The Incredible Hulk's alter ego is nuclear scientist Robert Bruce Banner, inventor of the gamma bomb, who absorbed a vast quantity of gamma rays after risking his life to save a teenager.
